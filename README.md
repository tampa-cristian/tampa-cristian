### 🧭 About Me

Senior Frontend Engineer with **8+ years** of teaching browsers manners and helping platforms behave under pressure. I design and ship scalable web systems built on **React**, **TypeScript**, and **cloud-native architectures**, the kind that quietly serve tens of thousands of users across regions, devices, and time zones without anyone noticing they exist.

I lead engineering teams, translate fuzzy business requirements into resilient architectures, and care about the boring stuff that keeps products alive at 4 PM on a Friday: testing, observability, and not deploying right before the weekend. Strong opinions about developer experience, mostly that we should have more of it.
